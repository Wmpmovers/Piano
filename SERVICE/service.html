Piano Disposal and Piano Donation.

Professional WMP Piano Movers and Storage understands that there might come a time where you might need to part with, or dispose of, your piano. If your piano is in need of disposal, rest assured that your piano will be going to help the less fortunate. The piano will be disassembled and the parts will be used to help fund mission trips. Along with your piano, other pianos are given from around the area. The more we are able to give, the more we are able to help. Not only do we appreciate your business, the people you are helping appreciate you! We also accept piano donations. To see if your piano qualifies to be donated we will need to know the serial number, model number along with pictures of the piano. If your piano qualifies for donation we will supply you with a donation form.
