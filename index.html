Below is a list of our professional piano moving services.

Piano Storage

Whether you want to store your piano short or long term, Professional WMP Piano Movers and Storage will look after your piano in our secure temperature controlled piano storage facility. Take as long as you need before we deliver it back to you.

Piano Tuning

Piano tuning is an essential part of keeping your piano in a great working condition - do it at least once a year. If you notice that your piano sounds “off” or feels like it needs an adjustment, give us a call!

Piano Repaire

Need help with a piano repair? Whether it’s for something minor or major, technical, restoring or cleaning, Professional Piano Movers and Storage will ensure your piano is repaired with care and quality. Call/Email us today for a quote!

Piano Disposal

Professional WMP Piano Movers and Storage understands the time may come for piano to be disposed or removed from the property. We offer a piano removing service no matter the condition.

Piano Donation

Email us today to see if your piano qualifies for donation - you must send pictures of serial and model number.

Piano Creating and Hoisting

Whether it’s crating for moving/shipping or piano craning for the move, Professional Piano Movers and Storage has the equipment and the experience to safely move your piano. Occasionally a piano will not fit up the steps and would need craned or hoisted in a window or onto a balcony.
